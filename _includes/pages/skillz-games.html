Intro

Skillz games are hard to find. But, not anymore!

Since Skillz isn’t a game itself, but rather a platform that goes into existing games, it makes finding a Skillz game rather tough.

That’s right! Skillz isn’t a game! Skillz is just a platform that goes into a mobile game that enables the players of that game to play against each other for money!
